**Impact:** This is QuillBot's biggest distribution moat. 4.5M Chrome extension users drive massive organic growth.

## 4. Development Plan - Phased Roadmap

### Phase 1: SEO Content Explosion (Weeks 1-4)
**Goal:** Massively increase organic search entry points

#### 1.1 Translation Pair SEO Pages
Create dynamic pages at `/translate/{source}-to-{target}/` for all language combinations.
- 52 languages × 51 targets = 2,652 potential pages
- Each page: custom H1, meta title/description, embedded translator tool, FAQ section
- Implementation: Single view + URL pattern with language validation
- **Files:** New URL patterns in `translator/urls.py`, new view in `translator/views.py`, new template `translator/pair.html`
- **Effort:** 1-2 days (template + view + URL routing, content auto-generated)

#### 1.2 Language-Specific Grammar Checker Pages
Create pages at `/german-grammar-check/`, `/french-grammar-check/`, `/spanish-grammar-check/`, `/portuguese-grammar-check/`, `/dutch-grammar-check/`
- Each page: localized H1/meta, same grammar checker tool, FAQ section in that language
- Implementation: New SEO pages in `seo/views.py`
- **Effort:** 1 day

#### 1.3 File Converter SEO Pages
Create individual pages for every conversion pair:
- Image: PNG→JPG, JPG→PNG, HEIC→JPG, WebP→PNG, etc. (~50-100 pages)
- Document: Word→PDF, PDF→Word, Excel→PDF, etc. (~30-50 pages)
- Each page: custom title, description, FAQ, same converter tool
- Implementation: Dynamic view in `media_tools/` or new `converters/` app
- **Effort:** 2-3 days

#### 1.4 Character Counter Page
Create `/character-counter/` as a separate page (currently bundled in word counter).
- Same JS logic, different template with character-focused framing
- Add social media character limits display (Twitter 280, Instagram 2200, etc.)
- Also add social media limits to existing word counter
- **Effort:** Half day

#### 1.5 Blog Content
Generate and publish 50+ initial blog posts covering:
- Writing tips & techniques (grammar rules, punctuation guides, essay writing)
- AI writing guides (how to use each tool)
- Comparison articles (WritingBot vs competitors)
- Language learning content
- SEO-driven content (word lists, quote collections, etc.)
- **Effort:** 1-2 weeks (can use AI to draft, human to review)

#### 1.6 Courses Content
Populate courses with 15-20 free writing courses:
- Introduction to College Writing
- English Composition I & II
- Introduction to Creative Writing
- Research-Based Writing
- Grammar Fundamentals
- Academic Writing
- Business Writing
- Essay Writing
- Citation & Research Skills
- Writing for the Web
- **Source:** OER content (Creative Commons licensed textbooks)
- **Effort:** 1-2 weeks

#### 1.7 Guides Section
Create `/guides/` with 5-10 long-form guides:
- How to Paraphrase (steps, examples, tips)
- Grammar Guide (common mistakes, rules)
- AI Writing Assistant Guide
- Citation Guide (APA, MLA, Chicago)
- Academic Writing Guide

### Phase 2: Core Tool Improvements (Weeks 3-6)

#### 2.1 Grammar Checker Enhancements
- **Writing Scores:** Add fluency, clarity, engagement, and delivery scores (0-100)
- **Tone Insights:** Analyze and display text tone (formal, casual, professional, friendly, confident)
- **Readability Metrics:** Flesch-Kincaid grade level, reading ease score
- **Fix-All Button:** One-click apply all suggested corrections
- **Passive Voice Detection:** Flag passive constructions with active alternatives
- **Files:** `grammar/services.py`, `grammar/views.py`, grammar checker JS/template
- **Effort:** 3-5 days

#### 2.2 Paraphraser Enhancements
- **Compare Modes:** Side-by-side view showing output from 2-3 different modes simultaneously
- **Color-Coded Diff:** Highlight changed words (red), unchanged sequences (blue), clickable synonyms (purple)
- **Inline Thesaurus:** Click any word in output to see contextual synonym dropdown (we already have the synonym API)
- **Files:** `paraphraser/` templates and JS, `static/js/paraphraser.js`
- **Effort:** 3-5 days

#### 2.3 Online Proofreader - Real Tool
Convert the SEO page into a genuine document proofreading tool:
- Upload document (DOCX, TXT, PDF)
- AI generates comprehensive report (error count by type, quality scores)
- Download corrected version of the document
- **Files:** New service in `grammar/services.py` or new `proofreader/` app
- **Effort:** 3-5 days

#### 2.4 AI Detector Improvements
- **4-Category Classification:** AI-generated, AI-generated & AI-refined, Human-written & AI-refined, Human-written
- **Bulk Upload:** Process multiple documents at once
- **Phrase-Level Explanations:** Show why specific text was flagged
- **Files:** `ai_detector/services.py`, detector template/JS
- **Effort:** 2-3 days

#### 2.5 Summarizer Improvements
- **Custom Mode:** Premium users provide custom instructions for summary style

- **Files:** `summarizer/services.py`, summarizer template/JS

#### 2.6 Word Counter Improvements
