Trust Center

Your data security and privacy are our top priorities.

Data Protection

  • No data training by default — We do not use your text inputs to train AI models unless you explicitly opt in.
  • Encrypted in transit — All data is encrypted using TLS 1.3 between your browser and our servers.
  • No text storage — Text submitted to our tools is processed in real-time and not permanently stored.
  • Secure file handling — Uploaded files (PDFs, images) are processed in memory and deleted immediately after.

Compliance

  • GDPR — We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation for EU users.
  • CCPA — We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information.

Security Infrastructure

  • HTTPS Everywhere — Our entire platform is served over HTTPS with Let's Encrypt SSL certificates.
  • PCI-Compliant Payments — All payments are processed through Stripe, PayPal, or Square — we never see or store your card details.
  • Rate Limiting — API and tool endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse.
  • Secure Authentication — Password hashing, email verification, and session management follow industry best practices.

Your Privacy Controls

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Contact our support team with any privacy questions
